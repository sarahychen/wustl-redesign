Majors and Minors for BSBAs

A wide rang of business majors...

To see more information about degree requirements, visit the Degree Requirements section of our website.

As an undergraduate pursuing your BS in business administration degree, you can choose from eight business majors:

  • Accounting
  • Economics and Strategy
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Finance
  • Healthcare Management
  • Leadership and Strategic Management
  • Marketing
  • Operations and Supply Chain Management

In addition to the programs listed above, you can also minor in

  • International Business
  • Business of Sport Management
  • Business of Entertainment

You can earn a second major or minor from one of the university's other schools, such as the College of Arts & Sciences or the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts. Similarly, if you are from another school in the university and wish to pursue a second major or minor in the Olin Business School, please refer to the Options for Nonbusiness Students section of our page.
